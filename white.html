Bio | Kevin White

Kevin White is the son of Kevin and Tammy White. He was born on June 25, 1992. He is an American football wide receiver. He played college football at West Virginia.

White played high school football at Emmaus High School in Emmaus, Pennsylvania. He then attended Lackawanna College for two seasons before attending West Virginia University. In his first season at West Virginia in 2013, he started nine of 11 games and had 35 receptions for 507 yards and five touchdowns. White returned his senior season in 2014 as a starter. He finished the season with 109 receptions for 1,447 yards and 10 touchdowns. He was named an All-American by numerous publications and was a finalist for the Fred Biletnikoff Award.
